Emergency help

If you think your account or device has been compromised, follow these prioritized steps:

  1. Disconnect the device from the network (turn off Wi‑Fi/cable).
  2. Change passwords from a trusted device and enable 2FA.
  3. Run a trusted antivirus or factory reset if malware is suspected.
  4. Contact your provider and, if necessary, local authorities.
